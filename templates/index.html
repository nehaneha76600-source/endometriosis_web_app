About Endometriosis

Endometriosis is a chronic gynecological condition where tissue similar to the lining of the uterus grows outside the uterus. It can cause severe pelvic pain, irregular menstrual cycles, infertility, and hormonal imbalance. Early detection significantly improves treatment outcomes and quality of life.
